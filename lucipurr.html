Lucipurr

Hey there, thanks for checking out "Lucipurr", one of my favorite songs that I've written. The track tells the story of a demon cat named Lucipurr who has the power to captivate and control those who come into contact with her. With her fiery gaze and alluring purr, Lucipurr is both fascinating and terrifying. She may seem friendly at first, but beware her sharp claws, as they can shred your skin in an instant. The chorus of the song emphasizes Lucipurr's seductive power over our souls. Her dark and twisted nature is both frightening and mesmerizing, drawing us in and confusing our minds.

Release date: March 1, 2023
